Empowering People Through Birth.

Our Mission

Mom’s Connection seeks to empower birthing people through positive pregnancy, labor, and childbirth experiences. Through empowerment and support we encourage birthing parents to advocate and educate themselves about all their birth and pregnancy options to create a plan tailored to the parent’s needs.

What’s A Doula?

A doula is a trained professional who provides continuous physical, emotional and informational support to a mother before, during and shortly after childbirth to help them achieve the healthiest, most satisfying experience possible.

Scientific Benefits

According to recent research, people who received continuous support were more likely to have spontaneous vaginal births and less likely to have any pain medication, epidurals, negative feelings about childbirth, vacuum or forceps-assisted births, and cesareans.
