Our Services

Mom’s Connection Doula Services supports hospital, birth center, and home births in the Knoxville area. We support all of your family’s birth and baby choices and will help your family gain understanding and confidence during this amazing and exciting time.

Our birth support includes:

  • VBACs and HBACs
  • Home births
  • Birth with or without pain medication
  • Water births
  • Support for singles, multiples, and larger families

We are well-acquainted with local hospitals and birth options and are ready to help you navigate them. We look forward to walking alongside your family every step of the way!

Health Insurance Coverage

There are a number of health insurance companies that cover birth doula services in their maternity care. Some of them are Tricare Prime, Tricare Select, and companies like BCBS, UHC, and WellCare. Check with your benefits manager or see if you qualify. If you have any questions, please reach out.
