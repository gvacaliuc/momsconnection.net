Hi, I’m Karen Vacaliuc

Hello! My name is Karen Vacaliuc, and I am a birth doula, connecting families through birth. I offer doula services in the Anderson, Knox, Morgan, and Roane Counties. I am passionate about mothers, babies, and family. I also have a deep desire to empower women through positive experiences of pregnancy, labor, and childbirth.

Our History

Mom’s Connection Doula Services was founded in 2019. Since then, we have supported countless parents on their journeys and remain committed to empowering birthing people and nurturing families. From small beginnings, our services have grown and we now proudly partner with a licensed massage therapist to offer additional comfort to pregnant parents.

DONA Certification

I have three adult children, all delivered by midwives, two with doula help. Reflecting on my own birth experiences convinced me to begin a certification process with DONA and I am so excited to offer my doula services to mothers and families in East Tennessee.
