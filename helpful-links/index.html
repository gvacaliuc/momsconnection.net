Popular Birth Contraction Apps

A few practical apps families often use during late pregnancy and labor.

Count the Kicks

Track how long it takes your baby to get to 10 movements and notice changes over time. Available in 20+ languages.

Contraction Timer App

Simple start/stop timer that calculates how far apart contractions are and how long they last.

Full Term

A basic iOS contraction timer with a bright, easy-to-use interface and contraction history.

Sprout Pregnancy

A pregnancy app with a kick counter and contraction timer built in.

Postpartum Support

Online support for postpartum, helps when folks say “how can I help?”

Give In Kind

Coordinate practical support after birth, including meals, errands, childcare, and other ways friends and family can help.

Take Them A Meal

Create a simple meal train so people can sign up for specific days and bring food without extra back-and-forth.
